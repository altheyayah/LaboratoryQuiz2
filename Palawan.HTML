Welcome to the Pearl of the Orient Seas

With its stunning land and seascapes, Palawan is a well-known tourist destination that provides unrivaled cultural, adventure, and natural experiences. Trekking through hills, valleys, and rivers, as well as snorkeling and diving in clean waters, are all available to visitors. In addition, a variety of festivals are held all year long, inviting guests to join in on the festivities and join the residents in their celebrations. Come and explore the absolutely amazing experiences Palawan has to offer!
